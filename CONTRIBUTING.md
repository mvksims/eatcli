# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in contributing.

## License Model

This project uses a dual-license model:

1. AGPL-3.0-only (open source)
2. Commercial licensing (separate agreement)

## CLA Requirement

All contributions require a Contributor License Agreement (CLA) before merge.

Reason: the CLA ensures the project can continue to be distributed under both
open-source and commercial terms.

## By Contributing, You Confirm

1. You wrote the contribution (or have the right to submit it).
2. You have authority to license your contribution.
3. You agree to sign the project CLA when requested.

## How To Submit

1. Open an issue (optional for small fixes, recommended for larger changes).
2. Open a PR with a clear description and tests where relevant.
3. Be available for review feedback.
4. Complete CLA step before final merge.
